Mission

We have designed an airline booking portal where any traveller can just signup or login and manage their flight bookings as per their journey dates.
Even if they forgot their password they can just reset the password through a confirmation rest password link.The main objective of this Project is to manage the details of airlines booking, passenger, ticket booking .The purpose of this project is to minimize the manual work for airlines booking .
